Basic Controls

A or a ---- move left
D or d ---- move right
S or s ---- move down
W or w ---- move up

Setup:

This project was built in Eclipse. Thus, the folder structure is:

rootFolder/src/ <all .java source files>
rootFolder/bin/ <all compiled .class files>
rootFolder/bin/Images/ <all .png images files>

Begin Playing
-Run the MainClass class.
-Expand the Window by dragging it with you mouse until all panels are visible, including the buttons/score display panel
-Use arrow keys and being playing
-After the Game Over screen pops up, you must click "Reset" button and resume playng
